Hit "Play all stages" or click any stage to watch a real subtitle line travel through six stages.
The ASR hears 美光 (Micron) as the homophone 沒光 and 密西根 (Michigan) as 密西跟 — errors like these get fixed at the LLM correction stage using on-screen OCR terms plus a learned term table, then the line is split to length and passes a quality gate before output.
